Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service.

üîç How do I report a lost item?

To report a lost item, click on "Report Item" on the homepage. Fill in the required details such as item description, location where it was lost, and your contact information. Once submitted, our team will review the report and assist you in the recovery process.

üîé How can I search for found items?

You can search for found items by visiting the "Found" section on our homepage. Use the search bar to filter items by category, date found, or keywords related to the item. If you find a match, you can contact the person who reported it.

üßæ Do I need proof to claim an item?

Yes, to claim an item, you must provide valid identification and any details that match the item description. This ensures that items are returned to their rightful owners.

üïí How long are items stored?

Lost items are stored for a period of 30 days. After this period, unclaimed items may be donated to local charities or discarded, depending on their condition.

üìû How can I contact support for further questions?

For any additional questions or support, you can reach out to us through the "Contact" page. Fill out the contact form, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

üí° What should I do if I find a lost item?

If you find a lost item, please report it using the "Found Item" section on our homepage. Provide details about the item and where you found it, so we can help reunite it with its owner.
