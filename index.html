How It Works

Our Campus Lost & Found System ensures lost items are easily reported and recovered. Here's how:

1. Report Item

Found or lost something? Fill out a quick form with item details and location.

2. Search or Browse

Use filters to search for reported lost or found items within the campus.

3. Reclaim with Proof

Contact the finder or submit identity proof to reclaim your lost item securely.
